How to get here?

From Cebu

Take a fast craft to Sibulan, Negros Oriental, proceed to Dumaguete City Port then book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Manila

Fly to Dumaguete via Cebu Pacific or Philippine Airlines. From Dumaguete Airport, travel by van or motorcab to Dumaguete Port, then book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Mindanao

From Dapitan City or Zamboanga City, take a ferry to Dumaguete City then book a ferry to Siquijor.
