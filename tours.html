Island Attractions

Beaches

Salagdoong Beach

Olang, Maria, Siquijor

Famous for its cliff diving spots and crystal-clear waters, this beach is perfect for thrill-seekers and those who love adventure.

Paliton Beach

Paliton, San Juan, Siquijor

Often compared to Boracay for its powdery white sand and stunning sunsets, this hidden gem is great for relaxation and photography.

Tubod Beach

Tubod, San Juan, Siquijor

Home to the Tubod Marine Sanctuary, this beach offers excellent snorkeling and diving spots, teeming with vibrant marine life.

Churches

St. Francis of Assisi Church (Siquijor Church)

Poblacion, Siquijor, Siquijor

Built in 1783, this historic church is the first landmark you'll see upon arriving at the port, known for its charming old bell tower and rustic coral stone walls.

St. Isidore de Labrador Church (Lazi Church)

Poblacion, Lazi, Siquijor

A UNESCO-listed heritage site, this grand church, built in 1884, is one of the oldest and biggest stone churches in the Philippines.

Our Lady of Divine Providence Church (Maria Church)

Poblacion, Maria, Siquijor

Known for housing the mysterious and eerie Black Maria statue, this church has a unique and intriguing history.

Nature & Adventure Spots

Mt. Bandilaan National Park

San Antonio, Siquijor, Siquijor

The highest peak in Siquijor, offering scenic views and nature trails.

Cambugahay Falls

Tigbawan, Lazi, Siquijor

A stunning three-tiered waterfall with turquoise waters, ideal for swimming and rope swinging.

Cantabon Cave

Cantabon, Siquijor, Siquijor

A thrilling cave adventure featuring stalactites, stalagmites, and underground streams.
